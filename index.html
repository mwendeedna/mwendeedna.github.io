About Me

My career didn't begin in analytics - It began with curiosity!

While working in data entry, I became fascinated by what happened to the information after it was collected. Who used it? What decisions did it influence? That curiosity became the foundation of my career.

That curiosity evolved into a career spanning Business Intelligence, Data Product Management, and Analytics Consulting, delivering solutions across FMCG, e-commerce, fintech, construction, media, marketing, and transportation.

Today, I design reliable analytics ecosystems—from improving data quality and automating reporting pipelines to building dashboards that empower stakeholders with trusted insights. I enjoy solving problems where the impact is measurable—reducing manual reporting, improving operational efficiency, strengthening data quality, and enabling faster, more confident decision-making.

For me, analytics isn't just about reporting on the past—it's about creating better processes for the future.

The Data Process Lab is my space for exploring how data, automation, and thoughtful process design can help organizations work smarter. Here, I share the projects, lessons, and systems I've built to turn complex data into practical business outcomes.
