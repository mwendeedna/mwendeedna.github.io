The Data Process Lab

The Data Process Lab is where I explore how data, automation, and thoughtful process design help organizations work smarter.

Here, I share the projects, lessons, and systems I've built to transform complex data into practical business outcomes. Every project featured here reflects my belief that great analytics isn't just about understanding data—it's about building better business processes through data.

How I Think

When I begin a project, I rarely start by asking which dashboard to build or which technology to use. I start by understanding the system.

What business problem are we actually trying to solve?

Before thinking about reports or dashboards, I want to understand the business objective and the outcome we're working toward.

How does information move through the business?

Understanding where data originates, how it flows, and where it breaks down often reveals opportunities that technology alone cannot solve.

Who relies on this information?

Every dashboard exists because someone needs to make a decision. I design solutions around people—not just data.

What can be simplified or automated?

Automation isn't about replacing people. It's about giving them more time to solve meaningful problems.

How will this solution scale?

A successful solution should continue creating value as the organization grows and its needs evolve.

"Tools evolve. Good thinking doesn't."

Every project in The Data Process Lab begins with understanding the system before selecting the solution.

Principles I Build By

🧩 Start with the Problem

Technology is rarely the starting point. Understanding the business is.

🔄 Follow the Data

Every metric has a journey. Understanding how information flows is just as important as analysing it.

👥 Design for People

The most valuable analytics solutions are the ones people actually use.

⚙️ Automate with Intention

Automation should remove repetitive work while increasing trust and consistency.

📈 Build for Tomorrow

Design solutions that continue creating value as the business evolves.

Why I Build Data Systems

My career didn't begin in analytics - It began with curiosity!

While working in data entry over 10 years ago, I became fascinated by what happened to the information after it was collected. Who used it? What decisions did it influence? That curiosity became the foundation of my career.

What began as curiosity evolved into a career spanning Business Intelligence, Data Product Management, and Analytics Consulting across FMCG, ecommerce, construction, transportation, media, and digital financial services.

Today, I design analytics ecosystems that organizations trust—from improving data quality and automating reporting pipelines to building dashboards that empower better decisions. I enjoy solving problems where the impact is measurable, whether that's reducing manual reporting, improving operational efficiency, or helping teams embrace data-driven ways of working.

To me, analytics isn't about reporting the past. It's about building better systems for the future.
