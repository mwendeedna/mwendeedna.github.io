The Data Process Lab

The Data Process Lab is where I explore how data, automation, and thoughtful process design help organizations work smarter.

Here, I share the projects, lessons, and systems I've built to transform complex data into practical business outcomes. My goal is simple: build efficient, scalable, and trustworthy data solutions that create measurable business impact.

Why I Build Data Systems

My career didn't begin in analytics - It began with curiosity!

While working in data entry over 10 years ago, I became fascinated by what happened to the information after it was collected. Who used it? What decisions did it influence? That curiosity became the foundation of my career.

What began as curiosity evolved into a career spanning Business Intelligence, Data Product Management, and Analytics Consulting across FMCG, ecommerce, construction, transportation, media, and digital financial services.

Today, I design analytics ecosystems that organizations trust—from improving data quality and automating reporting pipelines to building dashboards that empower better decisions. I enjoy solving problems where the impact is measurable, whether that's reducing manual reporting, improving operational efficiency, or helping teams embrace data-driven ways of working.

To me, analytics isn't about reporting the past. It's about building better systems for the future.
